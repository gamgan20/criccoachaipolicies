Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 15, 2025

Welcome to CricCoachAI (“we”, “us”, or “our”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the “Service”). Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data You Provide

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: When you register or authenticate via Firebase Authentication, we collect your email address and related authentication data.
  • Media Content: Images and videos you upload for analysis. These are temporarily processed via third-party AI providers (OpenAI and Anthropic).
  • Communications: Any correspondence you send to us, including support requests.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Service, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

  • Device type, operating system, app version
  • Log data such as IP address, timestamps, and crash reports
  • Usage analytics for performance monitoring and improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate and manage your user account securely
  • To process and provide AI-powered analysis of your uploaded media
  • To improve and maintain the quality and functionality of our Service
  • To communicate with you, including responding to support inquiries
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. Legal Bases for Processing Personal Data

If you are located in jurisdictions with data protection laws such as the EU’s GDPR, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), or similar regulations, our legal basis for processing your data includes:

  • Performance of a contract (to provide our Service to you)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate interests, such as improving our Service and security
  • Your consent, where required, for specific uses

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data only with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist with providing the Service, including:
    • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC): For user account authentication and management.
    • OpenAI and Anthropic: For AI analysis of uploaded images and videos. Media content is transmitted securely and is not retained beyond processing.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, legal process, or to protect rights.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, sale, or transfer of assets.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. Specifically:

  • Account and authentication data is retained until you delete your account.
  • Uploaded media content is processed temporarily and deleted immediately after analysis.
  • Aggregate and anonymized usage data may be retained for analytical purposes.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including but not limited to:

  • The right to access, correct, or update your personal data
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • The right to restrict or object to certain data processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent where applicable
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@criccoachai.com. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use third-party cookies. We may collect non-identifiable usage data internally to improve the app. Any tracking is limited to essential analytics and crash reporting and does not include personal identifiers.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where data protection laws may differ. We take appropriate safeguards, such as standard contractual clauses and strict security measures, to ensure your data is protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS), secure authentication via Firebase, and limited retention of uploaded media.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Effective Date above and notify you through the app or other means as appropriate. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
