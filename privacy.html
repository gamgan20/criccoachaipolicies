Privacy Policy for CricCoachAI

Effective Date: June 15, 2025

CricCoachAI ("we", "us", or "our") is dedicated to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use and protect it, and your rights in relation to your personal information when you use the CricCoachAI mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register or log in to CricCoachAI, we collect personal information necessary for authentication. This includes:

  • Email address

This information is handled securely through Firebase Authentication and is used strictly for identity verification and session management.

1.2 Media Content

When you upload an image or video for analysis, we temporarily collect and transmit that content to AI APIs provided by OpenAI and Anthropic. The media is used solely for processing and delivering your cricket technique feedback. The content is:

  • Not stored after processing
  • Not used for training or model improvement
  • Transmitted securely via HTTPS

1.3 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data such as device type, app version, and crash logs to improve performance and reliability. This information is not linked to your identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To analyze uploaded media and deliver AI-powered coaching feedback
  • To monitor and improve app performance and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share information with the following trusted service providers, and only to the extent necessary:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): for authentication and secure session management
  • OpenAI and Anthropic: for analyzing media submitted by users

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information as long as your account is active. Uploaded media is processed in real time and discarded after the session. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Transport Layer Security (TLS/HTTPS) for all data transmission
  • Access control mechanisms through Firebase Authentication
  • Limited, temporary media handling for analysis only

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent or object to processing

You can exercise these rights by contacting our support team at support@criccoachai.com.

7. Children's Privacy

CricCoachAI is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such data being collected, we will promptly delete it.

8. International Data Transfers

As our services may be accessed globally, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of your own. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data, consistent with applicable privacy laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law, or business practices. When we do, we will revise the “Effective Date” at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.
